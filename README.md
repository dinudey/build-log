# build-log

A living record of what I build, why it matters, and what it unlocks.

Auto-generated recaps run twice a week across all dinudey repos.
Monthly artifacts are written by hand from the best threads in those recaps.

## Structure

- `/recaps` — auto-generated every Wednesday and Sunday evening
- `/artifacts` — one hand-written artifact per month
- `/templates` — templates used by the routine and for artifact writing
